Simple Habits for Everyday Sustainability

You don't need a garden or a budget surplus to live sustainably. Small, consistent habits can make a big impact - especially when adopted early in life.

  • Bring your own bag, bottle, and cutlery.
  • Unplug chargers and electronics when not in use.
  • Buy local produce or split bulk purchases with roommates.
  • Donate or swap unused items instead of throwing them away.
  • Practice eco friendly student tips like meal prepping and energy saving.
